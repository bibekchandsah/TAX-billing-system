settings page
--------------
Business Settings Profile:
- Bill Title (editable, display in bill header)
- Business Name (editable, display in bill header)
- Business Address (editable, display in bill header)
- Business Contact (editable, display in bill header)
- PAN/VAT No. (editable, display in bill header)
- VAT % (editable, support up to 2 decimals, bydefault 13%) (max 2 numerical digit)
    this vat% will be used to calculate tax/vat% in bill page 

Unit category:
    list of all unit category (add/edit/delete unit category)
    this unit will display in bill page in unit column, and in add new particular modal in default unit dropdown

!!!
Fiscal Year Settings:
    Set the active fiscal year and configure the start/end months for your business year. In Nepal, the standard fiscal year runs from Shrawan to Ashadh. All records, dashboard stats, and reports will be filtered by the active fiscal year.
    |2079-80| |2080-81| |2081-82| |2082-83|

    Starting Month: (selected month in dropdown, bydefault Shrawan)
    Ending Month: (selected month in dropdown, bydefault Ashadh)

    auto add next 5 year from current year in grid, e.g if current year is 2082-83 then add 2083-84, 2084-85, 2085-86, 2086-87, 2087-88
